## Spain

Spain shows lower residual load during daytime periods.
This is likely caused by strong solar generation.

Residual load volatility is moderate.

## France

France presents more stable residual load behavior.

This stability may be related to the high nuclear share
in the French electricity mix.

## Germany

Germany shows the highest residual load volatility.

This may be caused by strong wind variability and
renewable intermittency.

Germany also exhibits large fluctuations across weeks.

## General Comparison

Among the three countries:

- Germany has the highest variability
- France is the most stable
- Spain benefits strongly from solar generation

Residual load patterns reflect different national
energy structures in Europe.
